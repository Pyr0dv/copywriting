Mini-Lesson
Imagine for a second what God thinks when he looks down and sees you...

Is he proud? Is he disappointed? Think of all you've done today so far.

If you were God, and saw this day, would YOU decide it was enough to reward you with massive success?

Or did you act like a loser and therefore deserve to live like one?

Call it God, call it the universe, call it whatever the fuck you think is best.

You only get what you've earned in this world, what you truly deserve.

If you don't have what you want, there is a reason.

Fix yourself
